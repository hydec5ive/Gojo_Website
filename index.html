About Me

Hi, my name is Harry. Some of my hobbies are playing basketball, swimming, working out, listening to music, watching anime/TV shows/movies, and playing video games. I’m from Shenzhen, China. Located in the southeast part of the country, Shenzhen sits right next to Hong Kong. It’s a global leader in technology and innovation, often dubbed the 'Silicon Valley of China.' Living in a place where groundbreaking ideas in electronics, AI, and blockchain come to life is truly inspiring. Once a small fishing village, Shenzhen has transformed into a renowned metropolis in just a few decades. Another standout feature is the food. Shenzhen offers a delicious array of Chinese cuisines, from spicy Sichuan dishes to fresh Cantonese delicacies like dim sum. I feel fortunate to have been born and raised in such a dynamic city.

In addition, I’m passionate about the blockchain world and cryptocurrency. The concepts of Web3 fascinate me—decentralized systems, smart contracts, and their role in shaping the future of finance and technology. I enjoy exploring emerging cryptos, tracking market trends, and understanding how blockchain innovations evolve as the financial landscape advances. It’s thrilling to discover new projects and imagine how they might disrupt the traditional system.
